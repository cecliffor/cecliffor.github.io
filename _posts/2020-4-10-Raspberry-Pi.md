The Rasperry Pi is a basic setup with OSMC installed, which was a basic install from the install wizard on first boot. This resulted in a fresh install of OSMC along with Kodi for managing my TV shows and Movie libraries.

My movies library is located on the external hard drive at mount point; 
`/media/Media_1/Movies/`

My TV shows library is located on the external hard drive at mount point; 
`/media/Media_1/TV Shows/`

After installing OSMC and pointing Kodi to the above libraries, I installed the Trakt.tv add-on to manage my watched status.
